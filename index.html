Victor Yidana

Full Stack Developer & Tech Innovator

Victor Yidana is the visionary developer behind YIDANA HOSTELS, a modern hostel management and booking platform designed to simplify student accommodation services in Ghana.

A Computer Science student at the University of Ghana, Victor combines strong technical knowledge with real-world innovation to build practical digital solutions. His passion for technology, entrepreneurship, and problem-solving led to the creation of YIDANA HOSTELS — a system built to improve transparency, efficiency, and user experience in hostel management.

Victor has hands-on experience in:

  • Web Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Backend Development (Node.js, Express, MongoDB)
  • PHP Development
  • Database Design & System Architecture
  • UI/UX Structuring for real-world applications

He has also earned multiple professional certificates from Coursera, strengthening his expertise in programming, system design, and modern software development practices.

Beyond academics, Victor actively participates in tech innovation programs and hackathons, constantly pushing his skills to higher levels. His mission is to build scalable, reliable, and impactful digital systems that solve real problems in Ghana and beyond.

YIDANA HOSTELS represents not just a project, but a vision — a step toward transforming hostel management through technology.
